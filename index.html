Flavours Updated

Flavour plays an important role in the popularity of cold drinks. It gives a refreshing taste and makes the drink enjoyable. Today, many types of flavours are available in the market, such as cola, orange, lemon, mango, and mixed fruit. Companies are also introducing new and unique flavours to attract young consumers. Some cold drinks are made with natural fruit extracts, while others use artificial flavouring. Recently, healthier options with low sugar and natural ingredients are becoming popular. Flavour not only improves taste but also creates a memorable experience for customers, making them choose the same drink again and again.
